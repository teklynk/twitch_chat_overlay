Twitch Chat Overlay

A clean chat overlay for your Twitch stream.

Features:

  • Multiple Themes: Choose between several themes including Cyberpunk (Orbitron) and Twitch Dark mode.
  • Customizable URL Params: Toggle badges, BetterTTV emotes, and chatter colors directly from the link.
  • Emote Support: Full support for native Twitch animated emotes and BetterTTV (Global & Channel) emotes.
  • Auto-Pruning: Automatically removes older messages from the DOM to maintain high performance during long streams.
